WHAT IS SOFTWARE ENGINEERING?

Whether you want to create a VR program for surgeons in training, the next “swipe right” dating app, or an autonomous car, software engineers have endless career options in today’s tech-enabled world. At Waterloo, the focus is not simply on writing code, instead, students will also have the opportunity to analyze software architecture, apply algorithms in development, understand digital hardware systems, and design human to computer interfaces. Plus, with an emphasis on collaboration, students will learn how to work in teams and work together on large scale projects - all useful skills once they enter the job market. Then, during co-op, students can put those skills to work at leading companies like Snapchat or Facebook or hot new startups. Upon graduation, students will be ready to create reliable, affordable, and faster software for all kinds of different purposes.

This is a 4-year course, with an additional 2 years of experience in the coop program. Tuition is $18 250 per year, for a 4-year total of $73 000.
