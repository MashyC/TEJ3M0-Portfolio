AREAS FOR SPECIALIZATION

Types of specialized jobs that can be obtained from a program in Software Engineering include:

Network Specialist

  • Network Specialists deal with the design, monitoring, installation, and repair of network hardware and software. The average salary in Canada ia $65 000.

Computer Architect

  • This concentration tends to highlight the engineering aspect of computer engineering. Professionals in this field will design and create physical computer equipment. The average salary in Canada is $75 000

Cyber Security

  • Cyber security is concerned with protecting networks and digital data as well as physical servers and computers from damage, attack, and unauthorized access. The average salary in Canada is $87 000.
